7th of July, 2026 - PLATA IS HERE

We proudly announce the compilation safn, a joint release from the newly founded label plata and legendary Smekkleysa. The album is a collection of some of the most exciting electronic music coming from Reykjavík at the moment.

The record is a collection of 9 songs ranging from contemporary club, refined art pop and experimental goodness. What links this collection of songs is a playful joy for- and approach to the creative process. The album will be available for purchase on August 13th.
